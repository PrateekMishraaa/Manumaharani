ABOUT US

Are you looking for a luxurious and peaceful retreat in the hills? Look no further than the Manu Maaharani Regency in Nainital! This exquisite hotel is nestled in the heart of the picturesque town of Nainital, surrounded by breathtaking views of the snow-capped Himalayas and the serene Naini Lake.
Manu Maharani Regency boasts a range of tastefully decorated rooms and suites, each one offering the perfect blend of comfort and luxury. From the cozy Standard Rooms to the spacious Family Suites, there is something for everyone here. Each room features modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi, and plush bedding to ensure a comfortable stay.
